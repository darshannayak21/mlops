Matchday Predictor

Pick the home side, pick the away side, and let the model call the winner.

This frontend is linked directly to your FastAPI backend. Enter the team playing at home, the visiting team, and the match date. The app compares both sides and shows the strongest winner signal.

What to Put

  • Home Team Type the club playing in its own stadium. Example: Arsenal
  • Away Team Type the visiting club. Example: Chelsea
  • Match Date Use the format YYYY-MM-DD, like 2026-05-01

Winner Form

Use exact team names from the suggestions. If the home and away teams are the same, the prediction will not run.

Prediction Result

Fill in the match details and click Predict Winner. The app will show the predicted winner and both teams' win probabilities here.
